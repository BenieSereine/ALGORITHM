About Us

Who we are

Kimenyi Aimable, Founder and CEO, did economics in high school with a goal to understand more how to build sustainable wealth; He did a double Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics and computer science at the University of Montreal with a deep need to master a combination of math, finance, and machine to automate managerial operations. At the end of 2007, he realized his continent business communities lack systems that listen to them; He came back to Rwanda and started prototyping small software to help a small business to manage daily transactions, named it Ishyiga.


Why choose us?

Ishyiga is a series of software each having its specific role, like a human body need different part which have specific task to perform, some clients use only one or few depending on the size of their needs, some use almost everyone of them responding to complexes need such as: “the full supply chain”, “full HR control and mobile remuneration”, “full financial and budget control”, “Full Fleet control”.

Our Business

Ineza Health Platform: Since 2017 we have 95% market of private pharmacies using our software Ishyiga POS, many asked us to create an easy way to save and share securely health data among them; Patient, Pharmacist, Doctor, Insurer, Wholesaler, Mtn; today with Ineza App you can ask for an appointment, search for drug and pay all your health needs.

Virtual Sales Data Controller: Since March 2019 we are collecting more than half of taxes for RRA in EBM project with this solution which integrate with taxpayers invoicing system and control each sale, stamp it and report it to RRA backend server every 15 seconds.

Virtual Claims Data Controller: Since January 2019, RSSB has approached us to conduct a study on how to make their claims verification done automatically, we have worked together to create and tested Ishyiga Symphony successful, now is waiting for board approval
